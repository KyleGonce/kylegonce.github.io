KYLE GONCE

ABOUT ME

A passionate storyteller

If you want someone who is creative, passionate, and can produce an inspirational story, Kyle Gonce is the guy. He is currently a student at Howard Community College studying Audio and Visual Media Production and his goal is to be a creative storyteller. As a child, and still today, Kyle is inspired by the stories underlying the videogames he enjoys. With a passion for writing stories, he self-published his first creative work in 2021, called “Hidden Realms.” This collection of short stories and poems features examples of his writing and interesting, out-of-the-box storytelling. In addition, Kyle created “Lyle vs Kyle” (2021-2022), a series of short sci-fi action-filled videos. He has experience with video editing software such as Premiere Pro and Wevideo, and is currently perfecting his Photoshop skills.

Kyle is equally comfortable onstage as well as behind the scenes. From public speaking to acting and improvisation, he thoroughly enjoys opportunities to entertain an audience. His professors and classmates often comment that he has a special talent in this field. Kyle also won an award for outstanding performance in theatre during high school. He has written for his high school newspaper, been an anchor for the school’s morning announcements, and has developed wonderful customer service skills while working at Harris Teeter. Even when he is not on the stage or at a podium, Kyle loves working with people and hopes his energetic personality helps to liven the workplace and makes the work fun.

No matter the project, Kyle enjoys entertaining and seeks to create his own wild and wacky stories. If you are looking for a creative story or an enjoyable entertainer, Kyle is the guy for you.

Kyle Gonce
